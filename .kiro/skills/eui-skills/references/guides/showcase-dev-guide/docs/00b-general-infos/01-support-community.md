# eUI Support and Community Interface

## Microsoft Teams Collaboration for Immediate Assistance and Community Interaction

The eUI team extends its support through a dedicated channel on Microsoft Teams, facilitating rapid assistance and community-driven queries. Interested parties with an EC email address can seamlessly access support via the [EC_eUI team on Microsoft Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/team/19%3a2f5bb6b7d1e24c4aabaa62229d3e1955%40thread.tacv2/conversations?groupId=fb6def72-c57b-4e8f-a82e-49be65d6e1f5&tenantId=b24c8b06-522c-46fe-9080-70926f8dddb1).

External participants, or 'Extra-muros' users, are advised to contact [DIGIT EUI SUPPORT](mailto:DIGIT-EUI-SUPPORT@ec.europa.eu) for guest access registration.

## Centralized Inquiry and Issue Resolution through Jira - Contact DIGIT EUI SUPPORT

For comprehensive handling of queries and support requests, stakeholders are encouraged to communicate directly with [DIGIT EUI SUPPORT](mailto:DIGIT-EUI-SUPPORT@ec.europa.eu) . When submitting bug reports, please ensure to provide a detailed account of the issue, inclusive of reproduction steps, sample code, the relevant DG/Project, and the eUI version currently in use.
