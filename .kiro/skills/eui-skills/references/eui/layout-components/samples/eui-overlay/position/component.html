The eui-overlay panel position property defines where to display the panel. By default, at the right of the screen.

Overlay panel available positions values:
  • right : sets the overlay position to the right of the screen (default)
  • center : sets the overlay position at center of the screen
  • top-center : sets the overlay position to the top center of the screen
Sample code:
<eui-overlay [isActive]="{{isOverlayActive}}" [position]="{{overlayPosition}}"></eui-overlay>
