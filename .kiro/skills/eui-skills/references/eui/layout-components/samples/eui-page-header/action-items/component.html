With page title, sub-title and action's button(s)

Page actions' buttons are always displayed to the right.

With long page title, sub-title and action's button(s)

It is highly recommended to display maximum 3 main actions' buttons and put other actions into the local menu (see hereafter). When in tablet/mobile mode, action buttons are automatically displayed into a local menu to enhance user experience and improve screen readability. Try to resize your browser to see it in action.

With actions and dropdown menu

The role of the local menu is to gather extra actions' buttons and custom links (optional).
